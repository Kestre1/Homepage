The job of a valiant knight is simple. Make heroic poses, valorous speeches, slash the sword a couple times, and bring back the head of a dragon! Or, at least, that's what Sir Hanson Grandious assumed, before he actually had to do it...

A short story written for a writing class in the Spring semester of the 2020-2021 school year.


Author's Note

I'm rather proud of this short story! As it was written as part of a writing class I took, I followed a process very different from my usual writing process. First, I wrote a draft of the story at the beginning of the class. Then I went through six weeks of writing course in which along with a usual assignment I would need to propose changes to my initial draft of this story. Finally, at the very end, I revised my previous draft using my own feedback and the feedback of my writing coach. Sure, I do revise, but this was probably the best revision process I've had in years!

The idea behind this story was just to create something of amusement that pertains to the ridiculous idea that knights in shining armor are a good thing. You'll see this a lot in other short stories set in medeival times. I hope it is as amusing to read as it was to write!
