A short story written for a school magazine in early 2021. Based on a previous, much shorter short story.


Author's Note
This short story is one I originally wrote in Spring 2019 as part of a "Short Short Fiction" class, which limited me to one page. Upon returning to it in early 2021, I decided to redraft it as a submission for a school anthology (from our Non-Locational Charter School a.k.a. homeschooling funder), this time with a requirement to stay under two pages. Since it was me, I just scraped by that requirement, but I'm rather proud that I was able to get anything under 1,000 words at all!

(And, in case you ask like my classmates, single quotes can be used to denote dialogue, and were used more commonly in 15th and 16th century literature)
