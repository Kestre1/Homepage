It has been two days since dragons left the Hidden World, and the Dragon Riders are hard at work, trying to help the dragons adjust. But when a terrible enemy shows itself, and old ways are reawakened, the thing Hiccup Horrendous Haddock III feared would come if dragons returned may come to pass.

Join the Dragon Riders as they discover dark secrets about the Land of the Dragons, and themselves, while the future of the dragons is once again at stake.

Book 2 in The Returning series


Author's Note
DISCLAIMER: Forget the quality of the previous book in this series, I even more do not endorse the quality of this book!

This is the second book in my very first series, not a full-length novel, but still a sizable story! This book, unlike the previous book, was written during the school year, which dramatically slowed down my writing process. Still, I had fun writing this book, since it represented a far more major emphasis on character development than I've ever done before. Even through hundreds of plot holes, almost 90% incorrect usage of "its" vs "it's", I think I can still declare this book a success, if a sloppy one!

This book took much longer, at 1 chapter a week for about seven or so months. Still, considering the fact that I wrote it while during my first semester of homeschooling, I think I did much better than I could've!
